About

The Poet Behind
the Pages

Jeremy Mason is a rising creative in the poetry landscape. A native of Arcadia, Louisiana, Jeremy has been writing poetry since high school and has independently published three poetry books. He is a lover of all arts including music, art museums, fashion, film, and certainly poetry.

"Dedicated 2 U" is the culmination of his works in his earlier years that highlight the strength of his family unit, while also tackling his love, his values, and his self worth.

Jeremy returned in 2020, during the pandemic and Black Lives Matter movement, with a profound book titled "The Life and Times of a Black Man." In this publication, Jeremy goes in depth about the experience of African Americans both in today's world and throughout history; in addition, he also delves into the psych of a Black male with the thoughts and emotions that they carry.

In 2023, Jeremy released his third book "Life is Beautiful." This time around, Jeremy highlights the beauty in life by speaking on both the ups and downs that make our existence meaningful. This book is full of insights on the world, religion, love, and loss.
